#### Claude Code: From Setup to Agentic Workflows | AICHR

- **Time:** 06:30 PM
- **Group:** 757 Artificial Intelligence Collective
- **Link:** [Event Link](https://www.meetup.com/aicollectivehr/events/313849897/)

**Description:**
***This event is capped at 500 attendees. Register now to secure your spot.***

**Claude Code: From Setup to Agentic Workflows**
Hands-on with the tool that's turning developers and power users into one-person engineering teams.

*This is a live, digital-only event. No in-person attendance. Join from anywhere.*

*April 9, 2026 · 6:30 PM EST*

AI coding assistants have been around for a while now. Most of them autocomplete your lines and call it a day. Claude Code is something different — it's an agentic system that lives in your terminal, reads your entire codebase, executes multi-file operations, and coordinates work across sessions and sub-agents.

This session is a live, demo-driven walkthrough of Claude Code and Anthropic's expanding agentic toolkit. We're not talking theory. We're opening terminals, pairing devices, and building things in real time.
Whether you're a developer looking to 10x your workflow or a technical professional who wants to understand what agentic coding actually looks like in practice, this is the session to attend.

Join Andrew Stafford for a hands-on tour of how Claude's tools work together to turn a single prompt into a coordinated, multi-step workflow.

🔍 **What We'll Cover**

**Claude Code: Setup & Core Concepts** Installation, configuration, and your first agentic session. How Claude Code reads your codebase, plans changes, and executes across files — not just autocomplete, but autonomous multi-step workflows.

**Tasks: From To-Dos to Orchestration** How Claude Code's Task system manages complex work with dependency graphs, filesystem persistence, and cross-session coordination. Break down big projects into parallelizable work that survives session restarts.

**Cowork: Agentic Power Beyond Code** Claude Code's capabilities applied to knowledge work on your desktop. Local file access, connectors to Slack, Notion, Google Drive, and more — all running in a sandboxed VM on your machine. Live demo of real workflow automation.

**Dispatch: Your Phone as a Remote Control** The newest feature in the toolkit. Pair your phone to your desktop Cowork session, fire off tasks from anywhere, and come back to finished work. We'll set it up live and put it through its paces.

**The Agentic Stack: How It All Fits Together** Claude Code in the terminal. Cowork on the desktop. Dispatch from your phone. Tasks coordinating across sessions. Sub-agents working in parallel. We'll map out how these pieces connect into a unified workflow and where they're heading.

🎙️ **Your Speaker**
Andrew Stafford Co-Lead of The AI Collective Hampton Roads 25+ years across technology, cloud architecture, and AI — including work with Amazon, NASA, healthcare platforms, and enterprise systems. Author of *AI for Business*. Currently building at the intersection of AI tooling, agentic workflows, and practical business automation.

Also runs [Claude Code for Business Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/claudecodeforbusiness)
And [AI Consulting Group](https://fractionalcaio.org/)

**Who should attend?**

* Developers and engineers who want to go beyond copilot-style autocomplete
* Technical professionals curious about agentic AI workflows
* Founders and operators looking to understand the productivity shift
* Anyone who builds, ships, or manages software and wants to see what's possible now

**Why this matters**
The gap between "AI assistant" and "AI agent" is closing fast. Claude Code doesn't just suggest code — it plans, executes, verifies, and coordinates. Cowork extends that to knowledge work. Dispatch makes it mobile. Tasks make it persistent.

This isn't a future roadmap talk. These tools are live, shipping, and available today. The question isn't whether agentic workflows will change how you work — it's how fast you'll adopt them.

Come see what it looks like when you stop chatting with AI and start delegating to it.
