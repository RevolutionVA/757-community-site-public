#### Claude as Your AI Coworker | AICHR

- **Time:** 06:30 PM
- **Group:** 757 Artificial Intelligence Collective
- **Link:** [Event Link](https://www.meetup.com/aicollectivehr/events/314079145/)

**Description:**
***This event is capped at 500 attendees. Register now to secure your spot.***

***Notice: This meeting will be recorded. By joining and remaining in this meeting, you consent to being recorded. If you do not consent, please do not join.***

Everyone thinks Claude Code is for developers. It's not.
Claude Code and Cowork are the closest things we have to a true AI coworker — one that connects to your email, your calendar, your files, and your apps, then actually does the work instead of just answering questions about it.
This session shows you 10 practical things Claude can do for you right now — no coding required. Every example follows the same pattern: you provide the idea and make the final call, the AI handles the grunt work, and you review before anything goes live. Human in the loop, every step.
This is our most accessible event yet. If you've been curious about AI but felt like everything was aimed at developers — this one's for you.

🔍 **What We'll Cover**

**10 Live Demonstrations (or as many as we can get done in the hour)**

1. **Daily Morning Briefing** — Calendar + email + priorities, generated before you wake up
2. **Meeting Prep Researcher** — Topic research + one-page briefing, ready before you walk in
3. **Email Triage Assistant** — Scan inbox, flag priorities, draft replies for your review
4. **File Organizer** — 500 messy files → sorted, renamed, organized in minutes
5. **Data Cleanup Specialist** — Messy spreadsheets → clean, standardized, formatted
6. **Competitive Research Analyst** — 5 competitors → structured landscape document
7. **Weekly Status Report Generator** — Compile your week's work into a summary
8. **Travel Planner** — Budget + dates + preferences → complete itinerary
9. **Subscription Audit** — Find and flag every unused recurring charge
10. **Presentation Generator** — Notes or a topic → branded slide deck

**The Human-in-the-Loop Pattern** Every demo follows the same principle: you bring the starting ideas and judgment. The AI does the heavy lifting. You review and approve before anything is final. The AI is the intern — you're the boss.

**Setup for Non-Technical People** We'll show you how to get Cowork running on your machine — no terminal, no code, no developer tools. Just the Claude Desktop app and your existing accounts.
